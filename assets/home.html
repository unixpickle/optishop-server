Welcome to Optishop!

Streamline your shopping experience by planning your route through the store ahead of time. Simply select your store, create a shopping list, and let our algorithms do the rest.

Get started by creating an account and finding your store.

Explore Your Store

Create your shopping list using products that are in stock in your store. Optishop knows about a store's inventory, and can even help you compare prices of similar products.

Visualize Your Route

Optishop combines a store's inventory data with a detailed floor plan to create a visual representation of your shopping trip.

Access from Anywhere

You can login to your Optishop account from multiple devices, making it possible to create a shopping list on your computer and see it later on your phone in the store.
